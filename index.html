Belur

Belur is home to the magnificent Chennakesava Temple, an architectural masterpiece of the Hoysala dynasty. The temple's detailed sculptures and carvings are a testament to the craftsmanship of the era.

Learn More

Halebeedu

Halebeedu is known for the Hoysaleswara Temple, built in the 12th century. The temple features intricate carvings depicting scenes from Hindu mythology.

Learn More

Yagachi Dam

Located near Belur, the Yagachi Dam is a popular spot for water sports and boating. It offers scenic views and a peaceful environment.

Learn More

Shravanabelagola

Shravanabelagola is one of the most important Jain pilgrimage sites, known for the colossal statue of Gommateshwara (Bahubali).

Learn More

Doddagaddavalli Temple

The Lakshmi Devi Temple at Doddagaddavalli, built in the 12th century, is one of the earliest Hoysala temples made of soapstone.

Learn More

Shettihalli Rosary Church

Built in the 1860s, this abandoned church is partially submerged in water during the monsoon season, creating a haunting yet beautiful sight.
